AI automation questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

What is an AI automation agency?

An AI automation agency builds custom systems that handle repetitive business tasks for you — checking portals, moving files, updating records, matching data, and chasing exceptions — using a mix of AI agents, scripts, and integrations with the tools your team already uses.

What kind of tasks can Yudi Labs automate?

Yudi Labs automates admin and workflow tasks like portal checks, document collection, report assembly, inbox cleanup, reconciliation, and exception alerts. If the task is repetitive, rule-based, and happens inside your existing software, there is a strong chance we can automate it.

Do I have to replace my current software?

No. Yudi Labs builds automations around the tools you already use. We do not replace your CRM, accounting software, or practice management system — we connect the gaps between them.

How long does an AI automation take to build?

Most single-workflow builds are delivered in two to four weeks, depending on complexity, access requirements, and the number of systems involved.

How is AI automation different from RPA?

Traditional RPA replays clicks and keystrokes. AI automation adds judgment — extracting data from messy documents, classifying exceptions, summarizing inbox messages, and adapting when interfaces change. Yudi Labs combines both where it makes sense.
