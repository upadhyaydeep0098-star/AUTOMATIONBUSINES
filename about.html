Why Yudi Labs exists

The story behind the agency.

Years working inside RBC, CIBC, BMO, and TD taught us one lesson: even sophisticated software leaves operational gaps. Skilled people end up filling those gaps manually. Yudi Labs fills them with AI automation instead.

Our founder spent years building automation systems for high-stakes financial teams — workflows where a single error meant regulatory exposure rather than a refund. That standard of precision shapes every Yudi Labs engagement.

The same pattern appeared in every business we observed — banks, clinics, logistics operations, property managers, finance teams: capable software handling the predictable path, and people quietly handling everything else. Reconciling reports. Collecting attachments. Checking the same portals each morning.

Yudi Labs brings that financial-services discipline to businesses that do not have a 200-person internal automation team — but lose just as many hours to the same repetitive admin work.
