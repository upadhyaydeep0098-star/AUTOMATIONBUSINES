Privacy Policy

How Yudi Labs collects, uses, and protects information. Last updated May 13, 2026.

1. Who we are

Yudi Labs ("we," "us," "our") is an AI automation agency operating from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through yudilabs.com and the services we provide.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to respond to inquiries and deliver our services:

  • Contact information you submit via forms (name, email address, task description, company details)
  • Communication records when you email, message, or call us
  • Technical information like IP address, browser type, and pages visited, via standard web analytics
  • Engagement information shared during projects (workflow details, system access scoped to the engagement)

3. How we use information

We use information only for these purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested services
  • Delivering and improving our AI automation builds
  • Sending occasional updates (only if you opt in)
  • Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. How we share information

We share information only with:

  • Service providers we use to operate the business (e.g., email, hosting, analytics) — under contractual confidentiality
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or business transfer

5. Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, or as required by law. Inquiry data is typically deleted within 24 months of the last interaction. Project data is retained per the engagement contract, then deleted or returned on request.

6. Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards — encryption in transit, access controls, audit logs, and least-privilege provisioning — to protect information under our control. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we treat your data with the same care we applied inside RBC, CIBC, BMO, and TD environments.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Withdraw consent for optional communications at any time
  • File a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@yudilabs.com.

8. Cookies and analytics

We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand traffic patterns (such as Plausible or Google Analytics). These tools collect anonymized information and do not personally identify visitors. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting site functionality.

9. Children's privacy

Yudi Labs provides services to businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

If you access our website or services from outside Canada, your information may be transferred to and processed in Canada. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, by direct notice.

12. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your information? Reach us at hello@yudilabs.com.
